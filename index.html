WHAT SHOULD I DO IF I GET A NOTICE OF VIOLATION?

1. SEARCH

Enter your notice of violation in our search box below

2. VERIFY

Watch and assess the video of the violation to determine whether it is your vehicle

3. VISIT

Once you have determined that it is indeed your vehicle, you may go to the Traffic and Parking Management Office

4. EXEMPTION

If you were giving way to emergency vehicles, an exemption would apply. Refer to FAQs or the help centre for further questions

VIOLATION RECORDED

Your vehicle was recorded by our traffic enforcement camera to have committed a violation against Ordinance No. 572 of Valenzuela City.

Please settle your violation within five (5) working days upon receipt of the violation. Failure to settle your violation within the prescribed period will be taken as an admission of guilt and shall be seen as a waiver of such right to contest the violation or present evidence as defense.

If you have any further questions, please refer to the FAQ
